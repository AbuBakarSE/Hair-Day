About Hair Day

Located in Madison, Hair Day is a boutique salon in cut, color, and styling.
Founded by Pria, a certified colorist who immediately specialized in edgy, custom coloring techniques, and her good friend Garry, a talented hairdresser.


We pride ourselves on our warm and relaxing atmosphere, creating a "treat yourself" environment for our clients. Our team of seven includes two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one makeup artist, all dedicated to helping you look and feel amazing.
